Habit Tracker

Habit Tracker is an application based off of the ideas in Steve Kamb's book: Level Up Your Life.

The idea is to live out the hero's journey yourself by becoming the character you want to be.

You are writing the story of your life, and this application is a tool to help you make that story a reality.

If you want to be Batman, this application will make you consider what you will need to do to become Batman in real life.

Choose a hero and set off on your journey!

My Character

A representation of where you are in your journey and how far you are from the final version of your character.

Currently under development

Goals

The skills and accomplishments the final version of your character has reached.

The idea is that whatever the final version of your character is capable of doing is what your goals will be. So go wild and aim high!

In addition to goals, you can add subgoals underneath a goal to break it up into more manageable pieces.

Quests

Tasks you complete to reach your goals. Each quest can be associated with a guild. The guild represents the category of quest and the type of work you will need to do.
